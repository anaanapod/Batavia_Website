Free Tours

Learn from the gallery experts and discover more detail about the murderous events that occurred around the Batavia Shipwreck. Offering audio, virtual and in person tours.

Audio Tours

The Audio Tour is self guided and allows you to explore the gallery at your own pace. We provide headphones and a tablet, so you may watch videos and view photos as you are learning.

Virtual Tours

Uncover the history of Batavia from the comfort of your own home. Steer though the gallery and select images and videos to reveal information about the shipwreck. Available on all devises.

In Person
